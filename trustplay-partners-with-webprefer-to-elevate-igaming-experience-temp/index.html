Trustplay Partners with Webprefer to Elevate iGaming Experience

Trustplay Technology Sweden AB Selects Webprefer AB's iGaming Solutions to Strengthen Player Experience and Engagement.

Sweden - Trustplay Technology Sweden AB, now under the ownership of Tangiamo Touch Technology AB, has chosen Webprefer AB as its provider for innovative iGaming solutions, solidifying its commitment to delivering enhanced player experiences. Trustplay has licensed Webprefer's latest version of Player Account Management (PAM) software, as well as a re-streaming solution designed to support live streaming and re- engagement initiatives.

Leveraging Webprefer's Advanced PAM Software

Trustplay has licensed Webprefer's newly developed PAM, a sophisticated software solution that supports essential backend processes, including secure gaming transactions, data compliance, and player account management. This updated PAM version builds on Webprefer's expertise from previous collaborations with Together Gaming and is tailored to meet the needs of various regulated markets. With PAM, Trustplay aims to centralize player data and streamline efforts around engagement, loyalty, and player satisfaction, a critical component in the competitive iGaming landscape.

Adopting Webprefer's Robust Re-Streaming Solution

Trustplay has also adopted Webprefer's advanced re-streaming solution, initially developed in partnership with Lead3 Media AB. This technology allows live stream broadcasting on platforms such as Twitch, YouTube, and Facebook while simultaneously integrating branded content into Operator's own channels. This re- streaming solution will support marketing activities focused on retention and reactivation, delivering a fully customized experience to players across platforms.

A Promising Collaboration

"We are thrilled to provide our proven technology to a forward-thinking company like Trustplay," said Mikael Lindberg Castell, founder and CEO of Webprefer and Lead3 Media. "This partnership highlights the versatility of our products and our commitment to bringing cutting-edge solutions to the iGaming industry. We look forward to seeing Trustplay leverage these technologies to drive growth and engagement.".

Webprefer's PAM and streaming solutions provide Trustplay with tools that enhance operational efficiency and facilitate seamless, secure gaming experiences. These additions are expected to play a vital role in supporting Trustplay's expansion and player engagement strategy within the evolving iGaming market.

About Webprefer AB

Webprefer AB is a leading technology provider specializing in iGaming solutions, including Player Account Management, live streaming software and Content Management System (CMS). With a focus on compliance, security, and user experience, Webprefer empowers operators to build stronger connections with their player bases and expand their reach across digital platforms.
