Add-On

Anywhere streaming server

Broadcast on multiple platforms.
Our own streaming server is currently configured to broadcast the stream on Twitch, Youtube, Facebook and of course the Gambitz site, which means that you may see the stream in any of these channels. Any new channel may also be added easily.
Branded streaming.
We also offer operators to implement our stream and chat on their own site in a simple way. This makes it possible for us to host dedicated ”branded” stream for operators and also feed it in our own channels.

Add-On

Game server

Game Server.
Our inhouse developed Game Server including three different games (Slot, Wheel and Double or Nothing). The games are used in the Live Stream by the host and members/players are able to spin these games in the show together with the host - perfect for retention/reactivation.
Control from the Host.
The games are loaded in the stream and the spin/select buttons are shown for the player in chat/stream area upon playing. Everything is controlled by the host from the backend during the stream and only those who are members and exists in our database and are online are able to play the games. Could be configured to pay result in Points, FreeSpins or Cash
